FROGGER!

Instructions --

This is your classic Frogger game! Use the arrow keys or W,A,S,D to hop across the grass without getting hit by the monsters!

Then, try to stay afloat on the logs to make it across the water. Getting to the other side of the water will complete the level and increase the difficulty.

If you are hit by a monster or unfortunately drown in the water, you lose one of your 3 lives. Hit 0 lives and game over!!

Have fun!!

P.S. Audio not supported in Internet Explorer (.wav files...) try another browser!
